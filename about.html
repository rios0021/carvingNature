ABOUT THE ARTIST

Gerardo Rios Corlay

Gerardo has been fascinated with all kinds of handicrafts from a very young age. He started with origami, as a young child, and developed his skills as he grew.

Although a robotics engineer by trade, Gerardo's drive and curiosity has motivated him to become a self-taught craftsman, investing his passion and perseverance in a variety of mediums.

His portfolio includes small sculptures, bone necklaces, brass carvings, bracelets, and antique weapons replicas.

Gerardo's artistry is uniquely crafted by hand, mostly -- using little machinery, which allows him to customize pieces for his discerning customers.

Gerardo loves to travel, and many of his distinctive pieces have been inspired by the cultures and diversity he has encountered on his adventures.
