POLICIES

Returns and exchange:

We provide 15 days of full refund on return policy. If you wish to return an item please contact us first.

If an order is cancelled before it is shipped it will be refunded immediately.

If the order is cancelled after it is shipped the package must be returned to us in original condition. The buyer may return items carefully packed within 15 days and will be responsible for the return shipping costs. The refund will be made once we have received the package.

Payment Policy:

We accept preferably PayPal, Visa, Mastercard.

If an order is cancelled before it is shipped it will be refunded immediately.

If the order is cancelled after it is shipped the package must be returned to us in original condition. The refund will be made once we have received the package.

Customs duties: If your local customs service charges import duties on the package these fees are the buyers responsibility.

Delivery Policy:

Once the payment has been processed, your items will be shipped within two business days. Items will be shipped from Japan to the address of destination via Japan Post.

Additional Information:

Your satisfaction is our first priority. We also appreciate your feedback and reviews if you are happy with your purchase.
